1.  Build the org.wso2.carbon.identity.provisioning.connector & copy the org.wso2.carbon.identity.provisioning.connector.inwebo-1.0.0.jar into <IS-HOME>/repository/components/dropins

3.  Follow the steps in https://docs.wso2.com/display/ISCONNECTORS/Configuring+Inwebo+Provisioning
