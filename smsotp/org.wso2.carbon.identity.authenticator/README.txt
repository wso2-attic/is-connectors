Steps to run

1.Build the org.wso2.carbon.identity.application.authentication.endpoint and copy the authenticationendpoint.war to wso2is-5.1.0-beta/repository/deployment/server/webapps/authenticationendpoint

Or unzip the authenticationendpoint.war and copy the smsotp.jsp from smsotp/org.wso2.carbon.identity.authenticator/src/main/resources to wso2is-5.1.0-beta/repository/deployment/server/webapps/authenticationendpoint

2.Build the org.wso2.carbon.identity.authenticator and copy the org.wso2.carbon.identity.authenticator.SMSOTP-1.0.0.jar to wso2is-5.1.0-beta/repository/components/dropins

3.Follow the steps in https://docs.wso2.com/pages/viewpage.action?title=SMSOTP%2BAuthenticator&spaceKey=ISCONNECTORS
